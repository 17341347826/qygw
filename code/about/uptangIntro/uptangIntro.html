COMPANY PROFILE

成都兴唐信息技术有限公司成立于2007年，公司总部坐落于成都高新技术产业开发区，历经多年， 已发展为集教育科技产品研发、销售、服务于一体的国家级高新技术企业。

在多年成长的历程中，公司始终按照"建设基于云计算和教育大数据云平台"的发展战略，不断吸纳 人才、整合技术及社会资源，多次荣获政府专项资金支持，公司获得了健康、稳定、快速的发展。 目前企业研发产品拥有多项国家专利和二十多项软件著作权。展望未来，我们将持之以恒打造最具 价值的教育生态圈，为中国的教育研究和改革保驾护航。

"以优质企业报社会"是我们兴唐人奋斗的目标和方向！

Established in 2007,Chengdu Xingtang Information Technology Co.Ltd. is located in Chengdu High-tech Industrial Development Zone .After many years ,it has been developed into a national high-tech enterprise integrating R&P,sales and service of education and technology products.

In the course of years of growth, the company has always absorbed talents,integrated technology and social resources according to the development strategy of “building a cloud platform based on cloud computing and big education data”,then gained special funds support of government many times and has achieved healthy,stable and rapid development. At present,the company's R&D products have a number of national patents and more than 20 software copyright.Looking forward to the future,we will continue to create the most valuable educational ecosystem, for protecting China's educational research and reform .

“To repay the society with high-quality enterprises ” is the goal and direction of the Xingtang people to strive for!
